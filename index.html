ABOUT ME

All Things Screen

I am a Irish born, New Zealand raised lover of all things film and tech. Design and film have been a dream of mine since I first saw Toy Story. Ever since I have surrounded myself in any and all forms of new and exciting technology opportunities and hope to continue doing so. I believe that great things are the result of a million little things done just right. I am also a somewhat rambunctious set of 3 penguins standing atop one another hiding in the clothes of a functional responsible adult.

Christopher Doran


Beginning with my first year of studying Design and Film at Victoria, I realised that I wanted to move into digital marketing and advertising. I got to work trying to get as much experience as I could during my university time to get myself ready to jump right in once I finished. I volunteered managing the social media facing side of a local café (DeVille Café Nelson), trying to get involved with marketing a product to a local community. I also started making short films to be entered in national festivals around New Zealand. Gaining a headlining position in Top of the South Film Festival and a runner up position in Tropfest NZ. In my last year of my design degree I was lucky enough to get involved with WeAreMouse.com, a digital marketing team that works with several agencies such as Y&R. This gave me time to work with a host of digital advertising types. Now having just finished my Design degree, and a Master in Design Innovation and a second degree in Film Production just around the corner, I continue to work with WeAreMouse and hope to find more opportunities in the wonderful world of web advertising.
